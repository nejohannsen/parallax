Edward J. Emering is a Chicago based photographer specializing in candid portraiture and macro photography. His photographic work has appeared in numerous magazines and journals, including SCUBA Times, Underwater Geographic, Trailprints and the Medal Collector. His work has also been exhibited in Chicago art galleries and museums. He currently has ten book titles in print, which feature his macro photography.

Ed believes strongly in the endless possibilities of capturing beauty with his camera. He has often remarked, "Beauty is all around us. Sometimes hiding in the most unlikely places. It just requires the skillful eye of the beholder to uncover it."

Dar Maziasz writes of his work, "Thanks for doing such a great job on our photographs. We all loved them and appreciated your patience."
