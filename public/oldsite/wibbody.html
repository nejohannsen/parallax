The WIB (Women in Black) is a career long project to capture black and white images of Chicago women. The goal is to illustrate, through Ed's photographs, the range of diversity Chicago women offer to the world. Sabrina, Mandy, Bridgette and Antje are just a few of the many women who will be featured in this work. The WIB Project is currently seeking inspirational Chicago women to be featured in this work. Simply call Ed at 312.245-9514 or email him at photographer@emering.com. Take your place in this work, or recommend a friend.
