Dollface, Model & Photographer

I first saw Dollface at Continuum during April 2008 when she participated in a demonstration of fire play. It was one of the most exhilarating exhibitions I have ever witnessed. I begged my dear friend, Miss Xena, to arrange an introduction, which she did. Dollface is an extremely mature, beautiful young woman and a pleasure with whom to collaborate. He has great poise and artistic sense and she brings it all to bear in her modeling. I feel blessed to have met her and I am proud to call her friend.
