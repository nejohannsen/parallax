Ann Marie, Actor/Model

I first met the ingénue, Ann Marie, on October 24, 2001. She was a fledgling photographer's model, who contacted me looking to build a portfolio of work from Chicago's leading art photographers. I had no idea what to expect during our first encounter. Much to my surprise Ann Marie was a vivacious, creative and hardworking model. The chemistry between us was strong immediate. We worked together very closely until I moved from the city in the fall of 2003. I have seen Ann Marie's modeling talent build and grow in leaps and bounds. Her willingness to pose in a variety of venues and guises has allowed me to produce some of my best professional photographic work. This gallery is a tribute to the range and diversity of our work together.

For more information about Ann Marie visit AnnMarieWeinert.com
