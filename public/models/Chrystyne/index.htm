Chrystyne, Artist/Model

My partner, E-lizabeth says, "Chrystyne gives good face." I would make it plural, faces. Chrystyne comes at you in ways neither you nor your camera lens can ignore. She is bold and she is beautiful. Since she was first referred to me in December 2002 by my good friend and highly admired fellow photographer, Jim Hewitt, she became a staple of my imagery. Such imagery has run the gamut from urban glamour to erotic fetish scenes. Whatever the venue, Chrystyne brings a high degree of professionalism and charisma to a project. I was pleased to call her model. I am even more pleased to call her friend. The chemistry, we share, is undeniable.
