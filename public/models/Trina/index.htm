Trina, Artist/Dancer/Model

It's hard to even begin to describe Trina. Alternative will do for starters. Goth may do as well. She has burst onto the scene as one of Chicagoland's hottest alternative models with her images being snapped up by Bizarre Magazine on her first try. She has also had several cover shots on Nude Freitags E-zine. She's a total natural and the camera loves her from nearly every angle imaginable. On top of which, working with her is fantastic. An accomplished artist in her own right and trained dancer, she brings a tremendous collaborative influence to her work. Trina also plays well with others.

She remains a close friend and collaborator to this very day.
